Mr Howard Yu

With over three decades in education, Mr Howard Yu brings rare insider knowledge to every lesson. As a former MOE Head of Department for Science and an official PSLE Science Marker, he understands exactly what examiners look for — and exactly how to help students get there.

"I don't just teach Science — I teach students how to think scientifically, answer precisely, and walk into any exam with confidence."

Mr Howard's approach combines structured concept mastery with targeted exam technique. Whether your child is preparing for PSLE Science, the AEIS entrance exam, or navigating the Upper Secondary sciences, lessons are designed to build genuine understanding — not just drill answers.

Small class sizes and individualised attention ensure every student is seen, heard, and guided at their own pace. Both in-person and online sessions are available.
